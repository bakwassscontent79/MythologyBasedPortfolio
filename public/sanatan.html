What is Sanatan?

Sanatan refers to the eternal, timeless principles that govern existence. It is not a religion but a way of understanding the fundamental truths of life, consciousness, and the universe.

Derived from the Sanskrit words "Sanat" (eternal) and "Ana" (without end), Sanatan represents the unchanging, perpetual wisdom that transcends time and space.
